Baking Tips

1.Butter your cream.

When creaming the butter, add air to the mix, which will help your cookies to puff up and rise when heated in the oven.


2.Select the correct pan liner

The pan and liner you utilize affects the visual and feel of your cookies.


3.Insert one egg at once

Add eggs slowly into the butter to inhibit the butter from breaking.


4.Take measures with caution

Mismeasuring your ingridients might lead to a disaster.
